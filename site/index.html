Om Studio

Heal your body and mind within our studio. Leave the class feeling realigned, relaxed and at peace. Om is not just a yoga class, it is a life changing experience.

Private Yoga

Our private yoga classes are all about you - your pace, your goals, your schedule. Work one-on-one with an expert Yoga teacher to learn the poses, techniques, and alignment that will best support and cater to your personal goals

Group Yoga

Our group yoga classes range from gentle to challenging, with relaxing, restorative classes. A great opportunity to get motivated, feel inspired, and build community.

Flying Yoga

Our flying yoga classes are fun and great for stress relief. They incorporates core strengthening, flexibility, alignment and floating meditations. Flying yoga is suitable for both beginner and advanced practionners.
